About Us

Our Head Office is based in Pretoria, where all products are manufactured. We are exclusively an online business, which means we need to courier your parcel. It costs R60 to a Pudo locker or R80 directly to your door.

We take around 7-10 working days to complete your order. Some teacher and business stamps can take up to 15 working days.

We are a small business, with a passion to produce excellent quality and beautiful products for you and your little one. We appreciate your support!
